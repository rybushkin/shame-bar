01 — About

Nobody plans to end up at Shame.
Everybody does.

It starts as one drink after work. A song you half-recognise. A stranger with a good opinion about something useless. Somewhere between the second round and the part you'll struggle to explain tomorrow, the night quietly becomes yours.

Shame isn't a place you visit. It's a place that happens to you. We keep the lights low, the records honest, and the door open later than your better judgment.
